Thabo Mosia born buttered and spiced in the not so dusty anymore streets of Soweto; Dobsonville to be precise.


Thabo also known as Kingtboz to his peers has a great resume attached to his name, including but not limited to the few successful start-up companies he was involved in founding and sometimes running the daily operations.


Thabo is currently the Co-founder, Director and COO of Rise African Child Media. He is an exceptional leader, a mentor and an impeccable creative director. A talent source with a one of a kind eye for talent and passion for arts and travels, hence the other moniker street kid. If you yearn for a fair balance between local is a lekker and international flare or formal with a hint of colloquial a jack and Master of all trades, then Thabo Mosia is your go-to guy.
