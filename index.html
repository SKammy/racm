Who We Are

Rise African Child Media (RACM) is a creative digital platform that aims to tell stories in the TV and film industry, stories that can change the enviroment. We want to educate and empower young upcoming entrepreneurs and film makers, and our goal is to create employment and help these young creatives to achieve and persue their dreams.
